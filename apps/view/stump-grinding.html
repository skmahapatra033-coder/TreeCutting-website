WHY GRIND STUMPS?

Fast, Efficient Stump Removal

Leftover tree stumps are not just unsightly—they're hazards. Stumps can cause tripping injuries, attract pests like termites and ants, and make mowing and landscaping difficult.

Our industrial-grade stump grinders can handle stumps of any size, from small shrubs to massive old-growth trees. We grind stumps 6-12 inches below ground level, leaving behind nutrient-rich sawdust that can be used as mulch or removed entirely.
